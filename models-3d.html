Why 3D Models

3D models have become essential across industries for design, planning, and analysis. Eagle60 delivers photogrammetry-grade models that improve real-world understanding across your workflow—without fixed-wing cost or delays.

  • High fidelity: sub-centimeter detail in ideal conditions for confident measurement.
  • Context: visualize terrain, structures, and surroundings in realistic 3D.
  • Speed: efficient capture and processing for timely decisions.

What You Get

  • Textured mesh (OBJ/FBX/GLTF) for BIM/CAD and visualization.
  • Point cloud (LAS/LAZ) for survey workflows.
  • Digital Surface Model (DSM) and orthomosaic for mapping.

How It’s Built

1) Plan & Capture

Structured flight plans with high-overlap nadir + oblique imagery and precise metadata.

2) Process

Photogrammetry pipeline generates point clouds, meshes, DSMs, and aligned ortho imagery.

3) Deliver

Export in your preferred formats for CAD/BIM, GIS, and 3D viewers.
